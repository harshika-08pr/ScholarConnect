Help center & FAQs

Find quick answers to common questions about using ScholarConnect.

How do I find scholarships that match me?

Create a free profile and add your class, course, category and income details. We use this information to show scholarships you are more likely to be eligible for.

Is ScholarConnect free for students?

Yes, ScholarConnect is always free for students. We do not charge for search, matching or applying.

Do you guarantee scholarship selection?

No, we only help you discover and apply to verified scholarships. The final selection is always done by the respective organisation.

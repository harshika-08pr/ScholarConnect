Our purpose

Making scholarships simple, clear and accessible.

ScholarConnect was built to solve a real problem: scholarships are confusing, scattered across different portals, and hard to understand. Our mission is to guide every student—from Class 9 to postgraduate level—towards verified, relevant scholarship opportunities.

What we do

We simplify the student financial aid journey through clarity and guidance.

Verified scholarship info

We collect and verify scholarship details from official government portals, foundations and universities to ensure accuracy and trust.

Personalised matching

Our eligibility quiz helps you discover opportunities that match your level, marks, income and study goals without confusion.

Simplified guidance

Clear eligibility, required documents, deadlines and application steps—explained in simple language for both students and parents.
